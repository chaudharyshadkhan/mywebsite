what is simple calculator

A simple calculator is a basic device or tool that performs fundamental arithmetic operations like addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. These calculators are easy to use and don't include advanced functions like square roots, logarithms, or trigonometry.

what is Timer

A timer is a device or tool used to measure and track time intervals. It can count up or down, depending on its purpose. Timers are commonly used in cooking, sports, studying, and various technical applications to keep track of durations and deadlines.

There are different types of timers:

    Analog Timers – Traditional timers with a mechanical dial.
    Digital Timers – Electronic timers with a digital display.
    Countdown Timers – Set to a specific duration and count down to zero.
    Stopwatches – Measure elapsed time from a starting point.
    Online/Software Timers – Found on websites or apps for convenience.
